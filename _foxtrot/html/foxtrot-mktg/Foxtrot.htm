What is Foxtrot?

Foxtrot is a cloud-based sofware solution designed to help flight schools with scheduling and maintenance reporting, while providing users with a centralized and secure location to store, download, or export data into various formats.

Included within Foxtrot is customer-centeric Front Desk software. Foxtrot enhances the Front Desk customer service software to provide operators with a more reliable, fluid and stable performance across multiple platforms.

What is Front desk?

Front Desk is mobile client management and scheduling software that serves many businesses, both large and small.

Front Desk software develops cloud-based and mobile client management software that gives business owners the freedom and flexibility they need to be successful and ultimately do what they love to do: run and grow their business.

How Will Foxtrot
Help My Business?

Foxtrot will help you keep track of the essentials of your flight school: pilots, aircraft, instructors, ratings, sign-offs, courses, lessons, student progress and much more.

Do you need to compare your students or find out which of your aircraft is under scheduled maintenance? Foxtrot allows you to produce custom analytical reports and keeps you on top of your schedule.

Is it User Friendly?

Yes, Foxtrot is user friendly. Our software utilizes the latest, intuitive design to boost functionality and performance. Managing large amounts of data has never been easier.
